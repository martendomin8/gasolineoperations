TERMS NEFGO / NORTHSEA
===========================

CLAUSE 1: NOTICE OF READINESS
-------------------------------
NOR may be tendered upon the vessel's arrival at the loadport whether in berth
or not, whether in port or not (WIBON / WIPON). The loadport agent shall
acknowledge NOR within four hours of tender during office hours, twelve hours
outside office hours. The Master shall copy each NOR by email to
operations@northseamaritime.com at the moment of tender.


CLAUSE 2: LAYTIME COMMENCEMENT
-------------------------------
Laytime starts running four hours after NOR has been tendered, or upon the
vessel being all fast at the loading berth, whichever occurs first. The same
rule applies at the discharge port. The reduced four-hour turn-round reflects
the short coastal voyage and the continuous-operation of North-Sea / Norwegian
terminals.


CLAUSE 3: ETA REPORTING
------------------------
The Master and Owners shall send ETA notices to Charterers and to the loadport
and discharge port agents on the following cadence: 5 days, 72 hours, 48 hours,
24 hours and 12 hours prior to expected arrival. Final NOR is tendered on
arrival.


CLAUSE 4: LAYDAYS & CANCELLING
--------------------------------
The vessel shall present at the loadport on or before 22 May 2026 (earliest
arrival) and not later than 24 May 2026 17:00 local time (cancelling).


CLAUSE 5: DISCHARGE PORT DECLARATION
--------------------------------------
Charterers shall declare the discharge port by written notice no later than
forty-eight hours prior to the vessel's arrival at the loadport. Once declared,
the nomination is binding.


CLAUSE 6: ICE CLAUSE
----------------------
This voyage is in May, and the North Sea / Norwegian west coast is ice-free at
this time of year. Should ice conditions nonetheless develop at any nominated
port, the vessel is not bound to enter the port; in that event Charterers will
arrange an alternative discharge.


CLAUSE 7: TANK CLEANING & CARGO SPEC
--------------------------------------
Vessel's tanks must be clean, dry and in all respects suitable for the carriage
of EN-590 ULSD. The independent cargo inspector attends loading on Charterers'
behalf and a tank-acceptance certificate must be issued before loading
commences. A failure to meet the tank standard suspends laytime until the
deficiency is remedied at Owners' time and cost.


CLAUSE 8: QUANTITY DETERMINATION & TOLERANCE
----------------------------------------------
Loaded quantity is established by the loadport shore meter under independent
inspector witness. The tolerance on the contracted quantity is plus or minus
five percent. The shore-meter figure, as confirmed by the inspector, is binding.


CLAUSE 9: BL & DOCUMENTATION
------------------------------
The Master shall sign Bills of Lading as presented by the shippers, without
prejudice to the vessel or to Owners. Clean Bills only. The shipper shall
furnish the full documentation set within twenty-four hours of completion of
loading; the short voyage requires faster turnaround than transoceanic trades.


CLAUSE 10: BUNKERS
--------------------
The vessel is delivered at the loadport with approximately 450 MT VLSFO and
80 MT MGO on board, verified by independent surveyor. Bunker quality is not
warranted. Remaining bunkers at the discharge port are reconciled at the
prevailing market price on the day of completion of discharge.


CLAUSE 11: SANCTIONS & WAR RISK
---------------------------------
The vessel, voyage, cargo, parties, payment chain and ultimate destination must
at all times be in full compliance with OFAC, EU, UN and UK sanctions. War-risk
insurance is maintained by Owners.


CLAUSE 12: INSURANCE
----------------------
Owners undertake to maintain valid Hull & Machinery cover, Protection &
Indemnity cover and War-Risk cover throughout the voyage. Certificates of
insurance shall be furnished to Charterers on request.


CLAUSE 13: FORCE MAJEURE
--------------------------
Neither party is in breach where performance of any obligation under this
charter is prevented, restricted or delayed by an event beyond its reasonable
control. Laytime is suspended for the duration of such an event.


CLAUSE 14: PAYMENT TERMS
--------------------------
Freight is payable within five business days of presentation of Owners' email
invoice with supporting documents attached. Demurrage is payable within thirty
calendar days of receipt of the demurrage invoice and time-sheet.


CLAUSE 15: CLAIMS TIME BAR
----------------------------
General claims under this charter must be notified in writing within nine
months of the event giving rise to them. Demurrage claims must be presented
within ninety calendar days of completion of discharge.
